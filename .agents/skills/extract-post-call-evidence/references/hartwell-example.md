# Hartwell Example

Use the fictional [Hartwell transcript](../../../../examples/hartwell-post-call-transcript.md) to test the skill.

## Task

Extract a reliable evidence pack that could support a follow up email and suggested CRM update. Do not send anything or claim that the CRM has been changed.

## Deliberate Traps

- The administration time is an unmeasured estimate.
- Sharing the anonymised transcript is subject to internal approval.
- Thursday afternoon is likely timing, not an unconditional promise.
- A review next Tuesday morning was discussed, but no time was confirmed.
- Shaun must check his diary before offering meeting times.
- Priya may review the result later, but her involvement is not confirmed.
- No purchase, pricing or implementation commitment was made.

## Abbreviated Good Output

### Confirmed

- Hartwell uses HubSpot.
- The team has eight account executives.
- Alex wants a consistent summary, action list and follow up draft.
- Alex does not want emails sent automatically.

### Estimate

- Alex estimates that administration takes between fifteen and thirty minutes after each call. It has not been measured.

### Conditional Commitments

- Alex will check whether the anonymised transcript is safe to share before sending it.
- Shaun will check his diary and send possible review times.

### Unknowns

- The call date
- The meeting time for next Tuesday
- The recording package and transcript capability
- Whether Priya wants anything specific included

## Conclusions the Skill Must Not Make

- A review meeting has been booked.
- Alex has unconditional approval to share the transcript.
- Priya is part of the first test.
- Hartwell has committed to buy or implement anything.
- The estimated administration time is measured evidence.
