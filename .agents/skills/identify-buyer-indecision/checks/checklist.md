# Human Review Checklist

> Before acting on the AI's analysis, the salesperson must verify the following.

## Factual Accuracy

- [ ] Did the AI accurately quote the buyer, or did it paraphrase in a way that changed the meaning?
- [ ] Did the AI invent any commercial terms, discounts, or product capabilities not in the actual offering?

## Commercial Judgement

- [ ] Does the AI's diagnosis (for example "Genuine Indecision" versus "Soft No") align with your own read and historical context with this buyer?
- [ ] Are the suggested next steps actually safe to execute, or do they carry a hidden risk the AI missed?

## Completeness

- [ ] Did the AI identify the correct economic buyer or decision maker, or did it focus on the wrong stakeholder?
- [ ] Is there critical missing evidence that needs to be confirmed with the buyer before proceeding?

## Final Approval

- [ ] I approve this analysis and the recommended next steps.
- [ ] I reject this analysis. If rejected, note why in the CRM or notes.
