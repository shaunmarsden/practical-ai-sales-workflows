## Consistent Post-Call Follow-Up for Northstar Analytics' Account Executive Team

### Overview

Northstar Analytics' eight account executives currently handle call notes, follow up and CRM updates inconsistently. CRM records can lag a call by a day or two, which reduces same-day visibility and makes the team's Monday pipeline meeting harder to run. A two-week pilot with three account executives tested a consistent process for turning call notes into a summary, an action list, a follow up draft and suggested CRM updates, with every output reviewed by the rep before anything is sent or saved.

### The Pilot Result

Three account executives ran the process against real discovery calls over two weeks. Two changes were measured directly, not estimated:

**Post-call admin time.** Before the pilot, the three reps' own timed tracking showed post-call admin taking close to twenty-five minutes per call. Running the same calls through the process brought that down to under ten minutes, measured by the reps themselves rather than guessed.

**Follow up email quality.** Drafts needed light edits rather than full rewrites, a meaningful difference from the team's current mix of individual approaches to writing up a call.

**Same-day CRM visibility.** The pilot's suggested CRM updates were available for review the same day as the call, addressing the delay that has been making the Monday pipeline meeting harder to run.

### What Changes With a Full Rollout

Extending the pilot from three account executives to all eight would apply the same process consistently across the team, rather than leaving it as an individual habit picked up by whoever piloted it. The Monday pipeline meeting would draw on same-day information across the whole team, not just the three who took part in the test.

### Commercial Proposal

The eight-seat team plan is £45 per seat per month, billed annually, for a total of £4,320 per year covering all eight account executives. This is the figure already shared and confirmed on the pilot review call.

### Data and Compliance

Call recordings and transcripts processed through this workflow are not stored or used to train any model beyond Northstar's own workspace. Written confirmation of this from the compliance team has been requested and is in progress; it has not yet been received; and it should be treated as outstanding until Northstar's IT lead has it in hand, not assumed to be already resolved.

### Time Commitment

No change to the reps' day-to-day workload beyond what the pilot already used. Rolling out to the remaining five account executives would need a single thirty-minute onboarding session per person to review the process.
