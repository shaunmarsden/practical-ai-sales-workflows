# Recipe: Hand Over an Opportunity

One job, one page. Everything you need is here; nothing else in the repository is required to use this.

| | |
| --- | --- |
| **Helps with** | Passing an opportunity to another person, team or stage without losing useful context or making the deal sound further along than it actually is |
| **You need** | Recent call evidence, CRM notes, relevant emails and agreed actions |
| **You'll get** | A short handover: a 30-second brief, the current position, people involved and their confirmed roles, evidence kept separate from assumptions, and a recommended next action |
| **Open** | [Workflow](../workflows/03-opportunity-handover.md) · [Prompt](../templates/opportunity-handover-prompt.md) · [Worked example](../examples/hartwell-opportunity-handover.md) |
| **The AI cannot decide** | Whether a stakeholder's interest is as firm as it reads in the notes, or what the receiving person still genuinely needs to check before taking this on |
| **You must check** | Someone could understand the current position in 30 seconds; every important claim traces to a source; tentative stakeholders are still described as tentative, not upgraded |
| **Then** | Talk it through with the receiving person, confirm they have accepted ownership of the next action, and correct any gaps together before it is treated as complete |

Want the fuller method or what a complete handover should actually contain? Open the [workflow](../workflows/03-opportunity-handover.md) itself.
